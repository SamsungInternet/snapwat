About Snapwat

Snapwat is a work-in-progress 'progressive web app' demo. The source code is shared at: github.com/samsunginternet/snapwat.

The app functions purely in your web browser (except for Twitter authentication if you choose to do that). If you do not choose to share your photos, they will remain only on your phone.

For questions, feedback etc, please contact the developer Peter O'Shaughnessy.
